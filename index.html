The Ginchiest Place For The Ginchiest Stay

For ten years we’ve been living it up here at The Ginchiest, where mid-modern meets new wave for all of your aestheticly pleasing & escapist needs. Rest in retro and make your reservation today.

Cinema

Throw back and indulge in some of the best that classic American cinema has to offer, from Rebel Without A Cause, to Roman Holiday. Our monthly screenings are sure to have something for everyone.

Dining

Avoid the stress of eating out on the town and have one on the city with us. Have dinner and a drink at The Passion Pit—our authentic styled diner with a modern late-night twist.

Listening Room

Movies not your thing? Tune into our listening room for some vintage rock and roll, and enjoy the music that made the 50s worth remembering.

Concierge

Earn more with your stay when you use our exclusive offers to enjoy the most of what Hollywood has to offer. Visit our concierge to plan your perfect pit stops, like touring the Hollywood Walk of Fame, and exploring the local nightlife.
